Meet ThermieChef

Hi, I'm Aly 👋

I'm Brazilian, I live in Australia, and I'm a little obsessed with one thing: making seriously good food ridiculously easy. My Thermomix is my sous-chef — it weighs, blends, steams and cooks — so I get the joy of cooking and skip the fuss and the mountain of pans.

My favourite thing? Taking the dishes everyone dreams about — from the world's best chefs to the Brazilian classics I grew up on — and reimagining them for the Thermomix, so you can pull them off on a busy Tuesday and watch the whole table go quiet for a second.

Come cook with me — and whenever you're ready for a Thermomix of your own, I'm right here to help you choose. 🧡
