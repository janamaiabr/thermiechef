Berry & Banana Smoothie Bowl

A thick, spoonable berry smoothie bowl blended in seconds in the Thermomix — the perfect quick breakfast.

TM6 / TM7⏱️ Prep 5 min🍳 Cook 0 min🍽️ Serves 2📊 Breakfast

Thick enough to eat with a spoon, ready before the kettle boils. The Thermomix powers through frozen fruit so you get that creamy, scoopable smoothie-bowl texture without any melting.

Ingredients

  • 200 g frozen mixed berries
  • 1 banana (frozen for a thicker bowl)
  • 150 g Greek yoghurt
  • 100 g milk of your choice
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • To top: granola, fresh banana, berries, seeds

Thermomix method

  1. Place the frozen berries, banana, yoghurt, milk and honey in the mixing bowl.
  2. Blend 1 min / speed 9, using the spatula through the lid hole to help it along, until thick and creamy.
  3. Spoon into bowls and finish with granola, fresh fruit and seeds.

Aly's tips

  • Use a frozen banana for the thickest, most scoopable texture.
  • Too thick to blend? Add milk 20 g at a time. Too thin? Add a few more frozen berries.
  • Make it dairy-free with coconut yoghurt and your favourite plant milk.

Questions

How do you make a thick smoothie bowl in a Thermomix?

Use frozen fruit and as little liquid as possible, then blend at speed 9 and push down with the spatula. The power of the blades gives you a thick, spoonable bowl.

Can I prep this ahead?

Portion the frozen fruit into bags so you can tip a serve straight into the bowl each morning and blend in under a minute.
