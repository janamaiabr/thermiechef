Orange & Almond Cake

My Thermomix reimagining of Claudia Roden's orange & almond cake.

My Thermomix version of Claudia Roden's famous flourless orange & almond cake — impossibly moist and gluten-free.

TM6 / TM7⏱️ Prep 15 min🍳 Cook 50 min🍽️ Serves 10📊 Dessert

Claudia Roden's whole-orange almond cake is a legend — flourless, fragrant and so moist it barely keeps. The Thermomix softens the oranges and makes the batter, skin and all.

Ingredients

  • 🍊2 whole oranges
  • 🥜250 g almond meal
  • 🍬250 g caster sugar
  • 🥚6 eggs
  • 🥄1 tsp baking powder

Thermomix method

  1. Place the whole oranges in the bowl with 500 g water. Cook 30 min / 100°C / speed 1 until very soft, then drain and cool slightly.
  2. Blend the cooked oranges (skin and all) 20 sec / speed 6 to a smooth purée.
  3. Add the eggs, sugar, almond meal and baking powder. Mix 20 sec / speed 5.
  4. Pour into a lined 22 cm tin. Bake in a preheated oven at 170°C for 50 minutes, until set. (The Thermomix makes the batter; baking is done in your oven.)

Aly's tips

  • Using the whole orange, skin and all, gives the signature bittersweet depth.
  • Naturally gluten-free thanks to the almond meal.

Questions

Is this cake really flourless?

Yes — almond meal replaces flour, so it's gluten-free and beautifully moist.

Why boil the oranges first?

Softening them mellows the bitterness of the peel.
