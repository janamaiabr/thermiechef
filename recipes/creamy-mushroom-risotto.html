Creamy Mushroom Risotto

A rich, stir-free mushroom risotto your Thermomix makes from start to finish — no standing at the stove.

TM6 / TM7⏱️ Prep 10 min🍳 Cook 20 min🍽️ Serves 4📊 Dinner

This is the recipe that makes people fall in love with their Thermomix. No stirring, no babysitting the pot — you add everything, set it, and walk away. The result is the creamiest mushroom risotto, every single time.

Ingredients

  • 1 brown onion, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 30 g olive oil
  • 250 g button or Swiss brown mushrooms, sliced
  • 320 g arborio rice
  • 100 g dry white wine
  • 800 g vegetable stock (hot)
  • 50 g parmesan, grated, plus extra to serve
  • 30 g butter
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, to serve

Thermomix method

  1. Place onion and garlic in the mixing bowl and chop 4 sec / speed 5. Scrape down the sides.
  2. Add olive oil and sauté 3 min / 120°C / speed 1.
  3. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook 5 min / 120°C / Reverse / speed 1 so they soften without breaking up.
  4. Add the arborio rice and wine, then cook 2 min / 100°C / Reverse / speed 1 to toast the rice.
  5. Add the hot stock. Cook 16 min / 100°C / Reverse / speed 1, with the measuring cup off and the simmering basket resting on the lid to stop splatter.
  6. Add the parmesan and butter. Stir 20 sec / Reverse / speed 1. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Leave to rest 2 minutes with the lid on — it will thicken to perfect, creamy risotto. Serve with extra parmesan and parsley.

Aly's tips

  • Use hot stock so the cooking time stays accurate.
  • Like it looser? Stir through a splash of hot stock just before serving.
  • On the TM7, follow the guided cooking pace — the stir-free magic is the same.

Questions

Can I make mushroom risotto in a Thermomix without stirring?

Yes — that is the whole point. The Reverse blade gently moves the rice so you never stir. Just add everything and let it cook 16 minutes at 100°C.

Which rice is best for Thermomix risotto?

Arborio or carnaroli. Their high starch is what gives you that creamy finish without cream.

Does this work on the TM6 and TM7?

Yes, the settings are the same for both the TM6 and TM7.
