Beef Bourguignon

JC
Inspired by Julia ChildMy Thermomix reimagining of their beef bourguignon

My Thermomix version of Julia Child's beef bourguignon — a rich red-wine beef stew, low and slow in one bowl.

TM6 / TM7⏱️ Prep 20 min🍳 Cook 70 min🍽️ Serves 5📊 Dinner

Julia made this French classic the dish everyone wants to master. The Thermomix simmers it low and slow on Reverse so the beef goes meltingly tender — no babysitting the pot.

Ingredients

  • 🧅1 brown onion, halved
  • 🧄2 garlic cloves
  • 🥓150 g bacon lardons
  • 🫒30 g olive oil
  • 🥩800 g beef chuck, cubed
  • 🌾30 g plain flour
  • 🍷300 g red wine
  • 🥩300 g beef stock
  • 🍅1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 🥄1 bay leaf
  • 🌿2 sprigs thyme
  • 🍄200 g mushrooms
  • 🧅200 g baby onions
  • 🫑Salt and pepper

Thermomix method

  1. Place onion and garlic in the mixing bowl and chop 4 sec / speed 5. Scrape down.
  2. Add the olive oil and bacon and sauté 4 min / 120°C / speed 1.
  3. Add the beef and cook 5 min / 120°C / Reverse / speed 1 to seal.
  4. Add the flour and cook 1 min / 100°C / Reverse / speed 1 to coat.
  5. Add the wine, stock, tomato paste, bay and thyme. Cook 60 min / 100°C / Reverse / speed 1, MC off and basket on the lid, adding the mushrooms and baby onions for the last 20 minutes.
  6. Season and rest. Serve with mash or crusty bread

Aly's tips

  • For deeper flavour, brown the beef in a hot pan first if you have time.
  • Even better the next day.

Questions

Can the Thermomix make beef bourguignon?

Yes — it sautés, seals and simmers low-and-slow on Reverse so the beef turns tender without stirring.

Best cut of beef?

Chuck — its connective tissue melts during the slow cook.

Is this the original Julia Child recipe?

No. This is Chef Aly’s independent Thermomix reinterpretation inspired by Julia Child and beef bourguignon; it is not endorsed by or affiliated with the chef.
